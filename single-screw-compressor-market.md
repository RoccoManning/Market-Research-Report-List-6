<p><p>Single screw compressors are categorized into two main types based on their mobility: stationary and portable. Stationary single screw compressors are typically larger in size and are permanently installed at a fixed location for industrial applications. On the other hand, portable single screw compressors are smaller and designed to be easily moved from one location to another. They are commonly used for construction, automotive repair, and other mobile applications where compressed air is needed on the go.</p></p>
