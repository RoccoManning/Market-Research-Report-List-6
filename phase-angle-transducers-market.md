<p><p>The Phase Angle Transducers market is categorized into AC and DC phase angle transducers. AC phase angle transducers measure the phase difference in alternating current signals, commonly used in power monitoring and control applications. They provide vital information for analyzing power factor and system stability. Conversely, DC phase angle transducers gauge the phase relationship in direct current circuits. These devices are essential in applications requiring precision in timing and synchronization of DC signals, such as in motor control and automation systems.</p></p>
