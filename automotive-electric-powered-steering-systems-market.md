<p><p>Automotive Electric Powered Steering Systems come in four main types: Column Type, Pinion-and-rack Type, Double pinion Type, and Ball screw Type. Column Type systems are integrated into the steering column, providing compactness and easy installation. Pinion-and-rack Type systems use a gear mechanism for precise steering control. Double pinion Type offers increased power assist for heavier vehicles. Ball screw Type systems utilize a ball screw mechanism for smoother and more responsive steering. Each type of system offers unique benefits suited for different vehicle applications.</p></p>
