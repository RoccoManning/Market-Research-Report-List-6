<p><p>C-Mount industrial camera lenses are available in two main types: fixed focal lens and zoom lens. Fixed focal lenses have a set focal length, providing a fixed field of view and focus distance. They are ideal for applications where the distance to the subject remains constant. On the other hand, zoom lenses offer adjustable focal lengths, allowing for variable magnification and field of view. They are commonly used in applications where the distance to the subject may vary, providing flexibility and versatility.</p></p>
