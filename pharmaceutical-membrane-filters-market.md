<p><p>Pharmaceutical membrane filters market is segmented into Microfiltration, Ultrafiltration, Nanofiltration, and others. Microfiltration involves using a filter with small pore sizes to separate particles from a liquid. Ultrafiltration is a process that separates larger molecules from smaller ones through a semi-permeable membrane. Nanofiltration is used to separate ions and small molecules from water. The "others" category includes specialized membrane filtration techniques not falling into the other categories, such as reverse osmosis and electrodialysis.</p></p>
