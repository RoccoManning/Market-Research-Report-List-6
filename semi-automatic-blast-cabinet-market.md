<p><p>Semi-Automatic Blast Cabinets are used for efficient and precise surface preparation in various industries. Pressure Blast Cabinets utilize compressed air to propel abrasive materials at high speeds, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Suction Blast Cabinets operate by creating a vacuum that sucks abrasive materials into the airstream for blasting. They are suitable for lighter applications and delicate parts. Both types offer semi-automatic operation, providing consistent results while minimizing operator fatigue and increasing productivity.</p></p>
