<p><p>Recyclable Aluminum Beverage Cans come in two main market types, Three-Piece Cans, and Two-Piece Cans. Three-Piece Cans are made by welding together three separate components - the body, top, and bottom. They are typically used for larger size cans and are more expensive to produce. Two-Piece Cans, on the other hand, are made from a single piece of aluminum, making them more cost-effective and suitable for smaller sizes. Both types are popular choices in the beverage industry due to their recyclability and sustainability.</p></p>
