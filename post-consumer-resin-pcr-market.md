The PCR market is categorized into several types based on resin composition: </p><p>- **PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)** is commonly used in beverage bottles. </p><p>- **PP (Polypropylene)** is found in packaging and containers. </p><p>- **HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene)** is used in milk jugs and detergent bottles. </p><p>- **LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene)** is often used in shopping bags. </p><p>- **Others** includes various recycled plastics like PS (Polystyrene) and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride). Each type addresses different recycling challenges and applications, driving sustainable practices in packaging and manufacturing.</p></p>
