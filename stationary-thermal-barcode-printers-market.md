<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary thermal barcode printers are available in two main types: direct thermal and thermal transfer. Direct thermal printers use heat to print directly onto thermal paper, making them ideal for short-term applications like shipping labels. Thermal transfer printers use a ribbon to transfer ink onto labels, making them more suitable for long-lasting or high-quality applications like product labeling. Both types of printers are commonly used in industries such as logistics, retail, and healthcare for printing barcode labels and other essential information.</p></p>
