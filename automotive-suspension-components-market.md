<p><p>The automotive suspension components market includes various types of components such as coil springs, leaf springs, stabilizer bars, suspension arms, and others. Coil springs are used to absorb shocks and maintain vehicle height, whereas leaf springs provide support and flexibility. Stabilizer bars help reduce body roll during cornering. Suspension arms connect the wheels to the chassis and control the movement of the wheels. Other components include shock absorbers, struts, and bushings that work together to enhance the vehicle's ride comfort and handling.</p></p>
