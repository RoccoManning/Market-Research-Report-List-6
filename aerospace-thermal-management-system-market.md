<p><p>Aerospace Thermal Management System market consists of two main types: Air-to-Air Thermal Transfer Type and Air-to-Liquid Thermal Transfer Type. The Air-to-Air Thermal Transfer Type systems transfer heat between two air streams without using any additional fluids, making them lighter and more efficient for some applications. On the other hand, Air-to-Liquid Thermal Transfer Type systems use liquid as the heat transfer medium, providing higher heat transfer rates and greater flexibility in design for more complex thermal management needs in aerospace applications.</p></p>
