<p><p>Digital Optical Micrometers are available in two main market types: Single-Axis and Mutli-Axis. Single-Axis micrometers are designed to measure one dimension or axis at a time, making them suitable for applications where only one measurement is needed. Mutli-Axis micrometers, on the other hand, are capable of measuring multiple dimensions simultaneously, offering increased efficiency and accuracy for complex measurements. Both types of micrometers provide precise measurements using advanced optical technology, catering to a wide range of industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
