<p><p>Vacuum coating systems are used in various industries for deposition of thin films on different materials. The market for vacuum coating systems can be classified into different types such as Evaporation, Sputtering, Ion Plating, CVD, and Others. Evaporation involves heating the coating material until it forms a vapor, which then condenses on the substrate. Sputtering uses high-energy ions to eject atoms from a target material onto the substrate. Ion plating involves bombardment of the coating material with ions for deposition. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) involves chemical reactions to deposit a thin film. Other types of vacuum coating systems may include PVD, ALD, and MBE.</p></p>
