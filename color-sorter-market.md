<p><p>The Color Sorter market is divided into two main types: Chute-Type and Belt-Type Color Sorters. Chute-Type Color Sorters use gravity to separate and sort materials based on color, while Belt-Type Color Sorters use a conveyor belt system for the same purpose. Chute-Type Color Sorters are typically used for larger and denser materials, while Belt-Type Color Sorters are more commonly used for smaller, lighter materials. Both types of sorters are essential in industries such as food processing, recycling, and mining for efficient and accurate sorting of materials.</p></p>
