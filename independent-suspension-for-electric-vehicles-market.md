<p><p>Independent suspension for electric vehicles allows each wheel to move up and down independently, improving traction, handling, and ride comfort. Multi-link suspension utilizes multiple arms to control wheel motion, providing precise control and handling. MacPherson strut suspension is a simpler design using a single suspension arm and strut assembly, ideal for cost-effective production. Transverse leaf-spring suspension uses a single leaf spring across the vehicle width, offering a lightweight and space-efficient option. Other types of independent suspension may include torsion bar, air, or magnetic systems for improved performance.</p></p>
