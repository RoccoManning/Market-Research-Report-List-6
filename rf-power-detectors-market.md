<p><p>RF Power Detectors are devices used to measure the power of radio frequency signals. There are two main types of RF Power Detectors: RMS Power Detectors and Non-RMS Power Detectors. </p><p>RMS Power Detectors provide a root mean square value of the input signal, which accurately represents the power level. </p><p>Non-RMS Power Detectors, on the other hand, do not consider the waveform of the signal and provide a more simplified measurement of power. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications depending on the requirements.</p></p>
