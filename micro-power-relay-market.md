<p><p>The micro power relay market is primarily divided into two types: closed type and open type. Closed type relays are enclosed in a protective casing, ensuring safety and reliability by preventing external interference and environmental factors from affecting performance. In contrast, open type relays lack this protective enclosure, offering more straightforward access for installation and maintenance, though they may be more susceptible to contamination. Both types cater to specific applications, balancing protection and accessibility based on user needs.</p></p>
