<p><p>Gas-fueled smokers come in different types based on their design and structure. Cabinet smokers are large, upright units that resemble traditional ovens, providing ample cooking space for smoking large quantities of meat. Vertical or cylinder smokers have a vertical design with multiple shelves for smoking food at different levels simultaneously. Offset firebox smokers have a separate firebox attached to the side, allowing for indirect heat and smoke to circulate around the meat for a longer cooking time and enhanced flavor.</p></p>
