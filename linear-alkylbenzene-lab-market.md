<p><p>Linear Alkylbenzene (LAB) is a key ingredient in the production of biodegradable detergents. The LAB market is categorized into different production methods. HF Paraffins utilize hydrofluoric acid as a catalyst for alkylation, producing high-purity LAB. AlCl3 Paraffins rely on aluminum chloride as a catalyst, offering cost-effective and efficient processing. Solid catalysts, on the other hand, provide an eco-friendlier approach with reduced emissions. HF-Olefins focus on high-quality olefin feedstocks, enhancing the overall performance of LAB production.</p></p>
