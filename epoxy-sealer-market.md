<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy sealer market can be categorized into two types based on their composition: water-based and solvent-based sealers. Water-based sealers are environmentally friendly, have low odor, and dry quickly. They are best suited for indoor applications. On the other hand, solvent-based sealers provide a more durable finish and are recommended for outdoor use or in areas with high foot traffic. Both types of sealers offer excellent adhesion, protection, and enhanced aesthetic appeal to the surface they are applied to.</p></p>
