<p><p>The handheld inkjet printer market is segmented into two primary types: USB and photoelectric interface printers. USB handheld inkjet printers connect directly to devices via USB ports, facilitating easy data transfer and integration with computers or mobile devices. In contrast, photoelectric interface printers utilize optical sensors to detect surfaces and trigger printing automatically, enhancing usability in diverse environments. Both types cater to varying user needs, emphasizing portability, convenience, and efficiency for tasks like labeling and marking in industrial or retail settings.</p></p>
