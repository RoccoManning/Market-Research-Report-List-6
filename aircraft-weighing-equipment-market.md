<p><strong>The Aircraft Weighing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform</li><li>Floor-standing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platform aircraft weighing equipment is designed with a platform on which the aircraft is positioned for weighing. This type of equipment is compact and easy to move, making it suitable for smaller aircraft. Floor-standing aircraft weighing equipment is larger and more robust, standing on the floor rather than being mounted on a platform. This type of equipment is better suited for weighing larger aircraft and is more stable and durable. Both types of equipment are essential for ensuring accurate weight measurements for aircraft maintenance and safety.</p></p>
