<p><p>Unit-dose packaging systems involve individually packaging each dose of medication, making it easy for patients to know when to take each dose. This system helps improve drug adherence by reducing the risk of missed doses. Multi-dose packaging systems, on the other hand, involve packaging multiple doses of medication together, often with compartments for each dose. This type of packaging is convenient for patients who take multiple medications and can help improve adherence by ensuring all medications are taken as prescribed.</p></p>
