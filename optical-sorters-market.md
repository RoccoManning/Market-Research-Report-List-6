<p><p>The optical sorters market includes two primary types: chute-type and belt-type color sorter machines. Chute-type color sorters are designed for bulk material, utilizing gravity to guide items through a detection system for precise sorting based on color and quality. Belt-type color sorters, on the other hand, use conveyor belts to transport products, allowing for versatile handling of various item sizes and shapes. Both types enhance efficiency in industries like agriculture, food processing, and recycling by improving product quality.</p></p>
