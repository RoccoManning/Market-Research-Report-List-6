<p><p>Automotive backup camera modules are devices installed in vehicles to provide a clear view of the area behind the vehicle while reversing or parking, helping to prevent collisions and increase safety. Technological advancements such as high-definition cameras, wide-angle lenses, night vision capabilities, and 360-degree surround view cameras are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the automotive backup camera modules market include the integration of these devices with other ADAS features such as parking sensors and automatic braking systems, as well as the development of wireless camera systems for easier installation.
