<p><p>RNA sequencing technologies market is divided into two main types: non-coding RNA sequencing and direct RNA sequencing. Non-coding RNA sequencing focuses on analyzing RNA molecules that are not translated into proteins, providing insights into gene regulation and functional genomics. Direct RNA sequencing allows for the sequencing of RNA molecules without the need for conversion into complementary DNA, offering advantages in studying RNA modifications and transcript isoforms. Both types of RNA sequencing technologies play a key role in advancing our understanding of gene expression and regulation.</p></p>
