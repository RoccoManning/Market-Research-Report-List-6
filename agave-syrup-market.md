<p><ul><li>Light</li><li>Dark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agave syrup is available in two main market types: light and dark. Light agave syrup is processed less than dark agave syrup, resulting in a lighter color, milder flavor, and higher fructose content. It is typically used in recipes where a subtle sweetness is desired, such as in beverages or baked goods. Dark agave syrup, on the other hand, undergoes longer processing which gives it a stronger flavor and darker color. It is often used in recipes that require a more intense sweetness, such as in marinades or glazes.</p></p>
