<p><p>RFID tags for livestock management are categorized into two main types: active and passive. Active RFID tags have a battery and can transmit signals over long distances, making them ideal for tracking larger herds or mobility. In contrast, passive RFID tags lack a power source and rely on the reader's energy for activation, making them more cost-effective and suitable for individual animal identification and health monitoring. Both types enhance traceability, efficiency, and overall management in livestock operations.</p></p>
