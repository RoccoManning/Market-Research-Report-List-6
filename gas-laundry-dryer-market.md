<p><p>Gas laundry dryers are available in two main market types: natural gas and liquid propane. Natural gas laundry dryers are connected to a gas line in the home, providing a consistent and reliable source of fuel. On the other hand, liquid propane laundry dryers use propane tanks to supply fuel, offering flexibility for homes without access to a natural gas line. Both types of gas laundry dryers offer efficient and cost-effective options for drying clothes.</p></p>
