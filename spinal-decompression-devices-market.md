<p><p>Spinal decompression devices are classified into two main market types: electric adjustment and manual adjustment. Electric adjustment devices utilize motorized systems to apply controlled traction to the spine, offering precise and consistent decompression tailored to patient needs. In contrast, manual adjustment devices rely on practitioner-operated mechanisms, allowing for hands-on control and adjustment during treatment sessions. Both types aim to relieve pressure on spinal discs and nerves, improving patient comfort and promoting healing in various spinal conditions.</p></p>
