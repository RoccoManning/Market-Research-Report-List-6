<p><strong>The Boat Trim System (BTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat Trim System (BTS) is a mechanism used to adjust the trim of a boat, improving its performance and stability. There are two main types of BTS in the market: Trim Tabs and Interceptors. Trim Tabs are attached to the stern of the boat and can be adjusted to change the hull's angle in the water, while Interceptors are adjustable surfaces integrated into the hull itself. Both systems help to optimize the boat's trim, reducing fuel consumption and improving overall handling.</p></p>
