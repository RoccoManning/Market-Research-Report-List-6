<p><p>Seismic Support Systems are essential for ensuring the stability and safety of structures during earthquakes. Three common types of support systems are Cylindrical Support, Bushing Support, and Conical Support. Cylindrical Support includes steel columns that provide vertical support, while Bushing Support involves rubber or neoprene bushings that absorb shock and vibrations. Conical Support features angled supports that distribute seismic forces. Each type has its unique benefits and applications in the construction industry to mitigate the impact of seismic events.</p></p>
