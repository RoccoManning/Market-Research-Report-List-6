<p><p>Automatic pool cleaning machines are categorized into three main types: Suction-side pool cleaners, which use the pool's filtration system to remove debris; Pressure-side pool cleaners, which utilize the pool's water pressure to dislodge and collect dirt and leaves, typically requiring a separate booster pump; and Electric pool cleaners, which are self-contained units powered by electricity, offering advanced features like programmable cleaning cycles and smart navigation. Each type serves distinct needs based on pool size, design, and maintenance preferences.</p></p>
