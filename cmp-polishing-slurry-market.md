<p><p>CMP polishing slurry is categorized into three main types: Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurry. Alumina slurry is widely used for polishing semiconductor substrates due to its high hardness and effectiveness in removing material. Colloidal silica slurry is known for its stability and uniformity, making it suitable for precision polishing applications. Ceria slurry is preferred for polishing optical components and hard materials like glass and ceramics, providing high material removal rates and superior surface quality.</p></p>
