<p><p>Ash coolers are used in power plants to cool hot ash generated from boilers. There are different types of ash coolers available in the market, including rotary ash coolers, drum ash coolers, and screw ash coolers. </p><p>Rotary ash coolers use a rotating drum to cool ash, while drum ash coolers use a stationary drum for the same purpose. Screw ash coolers utilize a screw conveyor to convey and cool ash. </p><p>Other types of ash coolers may include fluidized bed ash coolers or vibratory ash coolers, each with its unique cooling mechanism.</p></p>
