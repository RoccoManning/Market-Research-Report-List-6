<p><p>The semiconductor MEMS probe cards market is primarily segmented into vertical and cantilever types. Vertical MEMS probe cards feature probes arranged perpendicular to the substrate, offering high precision and stability, making them suitable for advanced semiconductor testing. In contrast, cantilever MEMS probe cards utilize a flexible, beam-like structure, allowing for a larger contact area and easier maintenance. Both types cater to the evolving demands of semiconductor testing, characterized by their unique strengths in performance, adaptability, and scalability in various applications.</p></p>
