<p><strong>The Porcelain Glaze Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Resin Coating</li><li>Water Soluble Resin Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Porcelain glaze coatings are generally classified into two main types: solvent-based resin coatings and water-soluble resin coatings. Solvent-based resin coatings are formulated using organic solvents as a base and are known for their durability and resistance to harsh environmental conditions. On the other hand, water-soluble resin coatings are environmentally friendly alternatives that are easier to clean up and have lower VOC emissions. Both types of coatings provide a protective layer for porcelain surfaces, enhancing their appearance and longevity.</p></p>
