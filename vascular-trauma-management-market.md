<p><p>Vascular trauma management market includes various types of procedures such as lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy, patch angioplasty, bypass graft, extraanatomic bypass, thrombectomy, and others. Lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy involves suturing of injured blood vessels. Patch angioplasty uses a patch to repair the damaged area of a blood vessel. Bypass graft involves redirecting blood flow around a blocked or damaged artery. Extraanatomic bypass creates a new route for blood flow. Thrombectomy is the removal of blood clots from a blood vessel. Other procedures include embolization and stenting.</p></p>
