<p><strong>The Copper Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Sintered</li><li>Medium Temperature Sintered</li><li>High Temperature Sintered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper paste market is categorized into three main types based on sintering temperature: low, medium, and high temperature. Low temperature sintered pastes typically bond at temperatures below 300°C, ideal for delicate electronic components. Medium temperature sintered pastes, used at around 300-600°C, balance performance and compatibility for various substrates. High temperature sintered pastes, requiring temperatures above 600°C, are suited for robust applications in high-performance electronic devices, offering superior thermal and electrical conductivity. Each type serves specific application needs in the electronics industry.</p></p>
