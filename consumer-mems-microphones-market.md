<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer MEMS microphones market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog MEMS microphones convert sound waves into analog electrical signals, offering simplicity and ease of integration in various applications. In contrast, digital MEMS microphones provide processed signals, often using I2S or PDM interfaces, which enhances performance by minimizing noise and improving sound quality. Both types cater to diverse consumer electronics, including smartphones, wearables, and smart home devices, catering to different performance and design needs.</p></p>
