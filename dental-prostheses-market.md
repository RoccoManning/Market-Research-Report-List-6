Composite materials offer a balance of aesthetics and durability, commonly used in veneers and fillings. Ceramic prostheses are renowned for their natural appearance and strength, making them ideal for crowns and bridges. Lastly, metal prostheses, typically made from precious or non-precious alloys, provide durability and are often used in dental implants and frameworks for prosthetic applications.</p></p>
