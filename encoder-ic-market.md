<p><p>The encoder IC market is segmented into linear and rotary encoder ICs, each serving distinct applications. Linear encoder ICs are used for measuring position and displacement in one-dimensional movement, often found in industrial automation and robotic systems. In contrast, rotary encoder ICs detect rotational position and angle, commonly utilized in motor control, robotics, and automotive applications. Both types are essential for precision measurement and control in various industries, driving growth in the encoder IC market as automation and smart technologies expand.</p></p>
