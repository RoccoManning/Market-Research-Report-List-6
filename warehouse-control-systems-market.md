<p><p>Warehouse Control Systems (WCS) are available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise systems are installed and managed locally within the warehouse facility, providing control over data security and customization. Cloud-Based systems, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for on-site maintenance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of warehouse operators, ensuring efficient management and operation of warehouse activities.</p></p>
