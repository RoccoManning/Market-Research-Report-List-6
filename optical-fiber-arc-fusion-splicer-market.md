Core alignment splicers use advanced technology to align the fiber cores perfectly, resulting in low splice loss. V-groove splicers align the fibers based on their outer cladding, providing a quick and efficient splicing process. Other types of splicers may use different alignment techniques or cater to specific needs within the industry. Each type offers unique features catering to different requirements in optical fiber splicing applications.</p></p>
