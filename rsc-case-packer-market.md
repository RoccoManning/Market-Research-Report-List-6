<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic Packer</li><li>Fully Automatic Packer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RSC Case Packer market is divided into two main types: Semi-automatic Packers and Fully Automatic Packers. Semi-automatic packers require some manual intervention in the packing process, such as loading cases onto the machine and removing filled cases. They are suitable for companies with lower production capacities. On the other hand, Fully Automatic Packers do not require any manual intervention and are ideal for high-speed production lines. They offer increased efficiency and productivity but are typically more expensive.</p></p>
