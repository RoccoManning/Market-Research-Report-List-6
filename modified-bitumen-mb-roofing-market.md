<p><p>Modified Bitumen (MB) roofing is a robust and flexible roofing solution, primarily categorized into two types: Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS) and Atactic Polypropylene (APP). SBS type offers enhanced elasticity and performance in low temperatures, making it suitable for various climates. In contrast, APP type provides superior UV resistance and durability, ideal for warmer environments. Both types are applied through torching, cold adhesive, or self-adhering methods, ensuring effective waterproofing and long-lasting protection for commercial and residential structures.</p></p>
