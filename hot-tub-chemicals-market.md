<p><p>Hot tub chemicals are divided into four main types: balancers, oxidizers, sanitizers, and others. Balancers help maintain the pH and alkalinity levels of the water, ensuring it is safe for use. Oxidizers break down organic compounds and contaminants in the water, keeping it clean. Sanitizers kill bacteria and other harmful microorganisms, preventing infections. Other chemicals may include clarifiers, enzymes, and algaecides, which help maintain water clarity and prevent the formation of algae. Different types of chemicals are essential for maintaining a clean and safe hot tub environment.</p></p>
