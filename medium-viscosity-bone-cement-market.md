<p><p>Medium Viscosity Bone Cement is a specialized material used in orthopedic and dental surgeries to secure implants, prosthetics, and other devices to bone. This type of cement is especially useful in procedures that require precision and minimal leakage.</p><p>The Medium Viscosity Bone Cement Market is poised for substantial growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of orthopedic surgeries, the rise in trauma incidents, and the growing geriatric population susceptible to bone-related disorders. Innovations such as antimicrobial properties and radiopacity in bone cements are also gaining traction, contributing to market expansion.
