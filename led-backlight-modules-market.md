<p><strong>The LED Backlight Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Lit RGB LED</li><li>Direct-Lit White LED</li><li>Edge-Lighted LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED backlight modules market encompasses three primary types: Direct-Lit RGB LED, Direct-Lit White LED, and Edge-Lighted LED. Direct-Lit RGB LED utilizes a full array of red, green, and blue LEDs, providing enhanced color accuracy and vibrant visuals, ideal for high-end displays. Direct-Lit White LED employs white LEDs positioned uniformly behind the screen, offering improved brightness and contrast. Edge-Lighted LED features LEDs placed around the panel's edges, allowing for slimmer designs and efficient space usage while still delivering adequate brightness.</p></p>
