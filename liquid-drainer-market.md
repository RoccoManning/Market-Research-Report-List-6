<p><strong>The Liquid Drainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Type</li><li>Guided Float Type</li><li>Inverted Bucket Style</li><li>Disc Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid drainer market types include float type, guided float type, inverted bucket style, and disc type. Float type liquid drainers use a float to open and close the drain valve based on liquid level. Guided float type drainers have a guided float mechanism for better stability and control. Inverted bucket style drainers use a bucket that rises and falls with liquid level to open and close the drain valve. Disc type drainers use a disc suspended in the liquid to control valve opening and closing based on liquid level.</p></p>
