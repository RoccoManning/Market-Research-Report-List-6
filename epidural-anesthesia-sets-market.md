<p><p>The epidural anesthesia sets market comprises two main types: regular epidural sets and combined spinal-epidural (CSE) sets. Regular epidural sets are designed for delivering anesthetics into the epidural space, primarily used for pain management during labor or surgery. In contrast, CSE sets combine both spinal and epidural techniques, allowing for faster onset of anesthesia and prolonged analgesia. This dual approach enhances pain relief efficacy and is increasingly favored in various surgical and labor settings, driving market growth.</p></p>
