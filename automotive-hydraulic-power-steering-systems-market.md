<p><ul><li>Pressure Hydraulic Power</li><li>Constant Flow Hydraulic Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Hydraulic Power Steering Systems can be classified into two main types: Pressure Hydraulic Power and Constant Flow Hydraulic Power systems. Pressure Hydraulic Power systems operate by increasing the pressure of the hydraulic fluid in response to the driver's steering inputs, providing variable assistance. On the other hand, Constant Flow Hydraulic Power systems maintain a consistent flow rate of hydraulic fluid regardless of steering inputs, offering a more stable steering feel. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing driver comfort and control in vehicles.</p></p>
