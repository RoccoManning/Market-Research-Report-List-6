PP packaging is known for its durability and resistance to high temperatures, while PS packaging offers clarity and rigidity. PE packaging is cost-effective and flexible, making it suitable for a wide range of products. PET packaging is lightweight and shatterproof, making it ideal for food and beverage applications. Each type of packaging material offers unique benefits for different packaging needs.</p></p>
