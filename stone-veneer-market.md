<p><p>Stone veneer market offers various types such as Ledgestone, Castle Stone, and Splitface. Ledgestone features a rugged, stacked appearance often used for creating a rustic or natural look. Castle Stone is characterized by its larger, more irregular shapes that mimic the appearance of traditional castle walls. Splitface stone has a rough texture created by splitting the natural stone, providing a more contemporary and modern aesthetic.
