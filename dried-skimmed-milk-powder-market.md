<p><p>Dried Skimmed Milk Powder (SMP) is classified into three types based on the heat treatment during processing- Low Heat SMP, Medium Heat SMP, and High Heat SMP. Low Heat SMP is minimally processed with a mild flavor and light color, suitable for use in products like infant formula. Medium Heat SMP has a slightly toasted flavor and is commonly used in baked goods and confectionery. High Heat SMP has a darker color and stronger flavor, often used in products like soups and sauces for a richer taste.</p></p>
