<p><p>Subsea production systems are designed to extract hydrocarbons from undersea wells and transport them to the surface. These systems include components such as wellheads, risers, and control systems. On the other hand, subsea processing systems are used to enhance production by separating and treating the extracted hydrocarbons underwater before they reach the surface. These systems can include technologies like subsea pumps, separators, and compressors. Both types of subsea systems play a crucial role in maximizing oil and gas recovery from offshore fields.</p></p>
