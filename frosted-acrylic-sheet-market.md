<p><p>Frosted Acrylic Sheet Market offers two main types: One-Sided Frosted and Double-Sided Frosted. One-Sided Frosted sheets have a frosted finish on only one side, allowing for light diffusion while maintaining clarity on the other side. Double-Sided Frosted sheets have a frosted finish on both sides, providing enhanced light diffusion and privacy. Both types are commonly used in applications such as signage, partitions, and lighting fixtures to create a sleek and modern aesthetic while diffusing light evenly.</p></p>
