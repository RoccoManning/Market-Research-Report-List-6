<p><p>Transcranial Electrical Stimulators are categorized based on the number of channels used for stimulation: Single-Channel, 2-Channel, and 4-Channel. Single-Channel devices deliver electrical currents to a specific area, making them suitable for targeted applications. 2-Channel stimulators enable simultaneous stimulation of two regions, enhancing the potential for complex brain modulation. Meanwhile, 4-Channel systems facilitate advanced research and clinical applications by allowing for more comprehensive brain network stimulation, providing varied and precise treatment options for neuropsychiatric conditions and cognitive enhancement.</p></p>
