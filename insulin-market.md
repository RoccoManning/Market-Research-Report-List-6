<p><p>Insulin market types can be categorized into animal insulin, regular human insulin, and insulin analogue. Animal insulin is derived from the pancreas of animals and is less commonly used now due to the availability of more advanced options. Regular human insulin is genetically identical to the insulin produced by the human body and is a common treatment for diabetes. Insulin analogues are synthetic insulins that have been modified to improve their pharmacokinetic properties, offering more precise dosing and better control over blood sugar levels.</p></p>
