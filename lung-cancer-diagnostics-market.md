<p><p>Lung cancer diagnostics market types include immunoassays, flow cytometry, rapid tests, molecular assays, tissue arrays, circulating tumor cells, pharmacodiagnostics, biomarkers, and other emerging technologies. Tissue arrays help in studying tumor samples. Circulating tumor cells can be isolated from blood samples for analysis. Pharmacodiagnostics help in determining the most effective treatment for individual patients based on their genetic makeup. Biomarkers are used to indicate the presence or progression of lung cancer. Other emerging technologies in the lung cancer diagnostics market continue to advance the field of early detection and personalized treatment.</p></p>
