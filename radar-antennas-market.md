<p><p>The Radar Antennas market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Half Wave Dipole antennas offer broad bandwidth and simple design, making them suitable for general radar systems. Quarter Wave Dipole antennas are compact and efficient, often used in portable applications. Uniform Linear Array Antennas deliver high-resolution imaging by focusing signals in multiple directions, ideal for advanced radar systems. Non-Uniform Array Antennas provide flexibility in gain and coverage, allowing for tailored performance in complex environments. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing radar capabilities.</p></p>
