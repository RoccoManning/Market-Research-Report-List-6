<p><p>Beer dispense systems come in three main types - direct draw system, air cooled system, and glycol cooled system. Direct draw systems are simple and commonly used in small establishments. Air cooled systems use a fan to circulate air around the beer lines, keeping them cool. Glycol cooled systems use a separate glycol chiller to cool the beer lines, making them ideal for long beer lines or multiple keg setups. Each type offers different benefits and is suitable for different types of establishments.</p></p>
