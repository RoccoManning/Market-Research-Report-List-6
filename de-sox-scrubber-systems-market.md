<p><p>De-SOx scrubber systems are used in industrial settings to remove sulfur oxides from exhaust gases. There are three main types of De-SOx scrubber systems: open loop, closed loop, and hybrid. Open loop systems discharge the scrubbed water into the environment after treatment. Closed loop systems recirculate the scrubbing water for continuous use. Hybrid systems combine elements of both open and closed loop systems. Each type of system has its own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
