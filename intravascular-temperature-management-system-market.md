<p><p>The Intravascular Temperature Management System market includes two main types: Intravascular Warming and Intravascular Cooling. Intravascular Warming systems are used to raise a patient's body temperature by circulating warm fluids through a catheter, while Intravascular Cooling systems lower body temperature by circulating cold fluids. Both systems are commonly used in medical settings to manage and regulate a patient's core body temperature during surgeries, post-cardiac arrest care, and other critical care situations.</p></p>
