<p><strong>The Flotation Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flotation Frothers</li><li>Flotation Promoters/Collectors</li><li>Flotation Depressants</li><li>Flotation Activators</li><li>Flotation Regulators</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flotation reagents market can be segmented into different types based on their functions. Flotation frothers are used to create stable froths on the surface of the pulp, while flotation promoters/collectors enhance the attachment of air bubbles to particles. Flotation depressants reduce the floatability of certain minerals, whereas flotation activators enhance the floatability of specific minerals. Flotation regulators are used to control the pH levels in the flotation process. Other types of reagents may include dispersants, flocculants, and buffering agents.</p></p>
