<p><strong>The Metallic Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Stamping Foil</li><li>Cold Stamping Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic foil market consists of two main types: hot stamping foil and cold stamping foil. Hot stamping foil is applied using heat and pressure to transfer a thin layer of metallic coating onto a surface. It is commonly used in printing and packaging applications. Cold stamping foil, on the other hand, does not require heat and can be applied directly onto surfaces, making it more suitable for a wider range of materials. Both types of foils offer unique decorative effects and are widely used in industries such as cosmetics, textiles, and electronics.</p></p>
