<p><ul><li>Non-Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives</li><li>Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PUR Hot-Melt adhesives can be categorized into two main types: Non-Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives and Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives. </p><p>Non-Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives do not require any additional curing process and provide an instant bond upon cooling, making them suitable for fast assembly applications. </p><p>On the other hand, Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives contain cross-linking agents that react with moisture in the air or substrates to form a durable bond, making them ideal for applications where high strength and chemical resistance are required.</p></p>
