<p><p>Rubber masterbatch market types include EPDM-based, SBR-based, NBR-based, and natural rubber-based types. EPDM-based masterbatch is known for its excellent weather resistance and electrical insulation properties. SBR-based masterbatch offers good abrasion resistance and elasticity. NBR-based masterbatch is valued for its oil and fuel resistance. Natural rubber-based masterbatch is derived from natural sources and is favored for its high resilience and tear strength. Each type caters to different industry needs and applications, providing a wide range of properties for various rubber products.</p></p>
