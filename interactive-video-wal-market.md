LED technology provides higher brightness and enhances color accuracy, making it ideal for large-scale environments. DLP (Digital Light Processing) utilizes micro-mirrors for superior image quality and is often employed in large venues due to its seamless projection capabilities. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying audience needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
