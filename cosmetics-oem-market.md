<p><ul><li>All process OEM</li><li>Half process OEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cosmetics OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) market includes two main types: All Process OEM and Half Process OEM. All Process OEM handles the entire manufacturing cycle, from product formulation to packaging, allowing brands to focus solely on marketing. Conversely, Half Process OEM takes on specific stages, such as formulation or packaging, leaving other processes to the brand. This flexibility allows brands to choose how much control they want over production while optimizing costs and resources in the cosmetics industry.</p></p>
