There are two main types of mould temperature controllers: oil type and water type. Oil type controllers are preferred for applications requiring high temperatures, while water type controllers are more commonly used for lower temperature requirements. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining consistent temperature control, improving product quality, and increasing production efficiency in the plastic manufacturing industry.</p></p>
