<p><p>Hair Transplantation Market Types include Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves the transfer of a strip of scalp containing hair follicles to the balding area, while FUE involves the extraction of individual follicular units from the donor area and implanting them into the balding area. Both methods are minimally invasive and offer natural-looking results, driving the growth of the hair transplantation market globally.</p></p>
