<p><p>The Electron Beam Machining (EBM) market encompasses several key applications, including welding, surface treatment, and drilling. EBM welding involves high-precision joining of materials using focused electron beams, ideal for thick and dissimilar materials. Surface treatment employs electron beams to enhance material properties, such as hardness and resistance to wear. Drilling utilizes concentrated beams to create high-precision holes in various materials, including metals and ceramics. Each application benefits from EBM's ability to deliver exceptional accuracy and minimal thermal distortion.</p></p>
