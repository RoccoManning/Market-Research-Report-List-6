<p><p>The throat cancer therapeutics market encompasses three primary treatment modalities: surgery, radiotherapy, and chemotherapy. Surgery involves the removal of tumors and affected tissues, aiming for a cure or symptom relief. Radiotherapy employs targeted radiation to destroy cancer cells or shrink tumors, often used post-surgery or as a primary treatment. Chemotherapy utilizes drugs to target rapidly dividing cancer cells, typically administered in cycles. These treatment types can be used alone or in combination, depending on the cancer stage and patient health.</p></p>
