<p><p>The Data Protection market encompasses various types of solutions to safeguard sensitive information. Data Backup and Recovery focuses on making copies of data to restore it in case of loss. Encryption and Tokenization protect data by encoding information. Data Loss Prevention (DLP) prevents unauthorized access or use of data. Identity and Access Management (IAM) controls user access to data. Compliance Management ensures adherence to regulations and standards.</p></p>
