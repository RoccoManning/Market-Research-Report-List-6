<p><p>The multimode fiber splitter market is segmented into various types based on the number of output ports. A 1×2 splitter divides a single input into two outputs, while a 1×4 offers four outputs from one input. The 1×8 splitter extends this to eight outputs. A 2×2 splitter provides two inputs with two outputs for increased versatility. Additionally, other types may include configurations such as 1×16 or 1×32, catering to diverse networking applications and scalability needs.</p></p>
