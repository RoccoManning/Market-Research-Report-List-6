<p><p>The bacterial identification system market comprises several methodologies. The antibiogram involves testing bacterial strains against various antibiotics to determine susceptibility, aiding in targeted treatments. MALDI-TOF (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization Time-of-Flight) is a rapid mass spectrometry technique that identifies bacteria by analyzing their unique protein profiles. Gram staining, a traditional method, classifies bacteria based on cell wall properties, distinguishing between Gram-positive and Gram-negative organisms. These methods enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment efficacy in clinical microbiology.</p></p>
