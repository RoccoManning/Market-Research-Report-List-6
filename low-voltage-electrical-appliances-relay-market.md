<p><p>The low voltage electrical appliances relay market is divided into two primary types: DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current). AC relays, on the other hand, are commonly found in household and industrial applications where alternating current is present, facilitating the control of devices like motors and lighting systems. Each type serves specific operational needs based on the current type.</p></p>
