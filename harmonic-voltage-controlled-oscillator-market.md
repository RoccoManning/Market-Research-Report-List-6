<p><p>The harmonic voltage-controlled oscillator (VCO) market can be categorized into three main types: LC-tank oscillators, crystal oscillators, and other oscillators. LC-tank oscillators utilize inductors and capacitors to produce a stable frequency, suitable for applications needing tunable and low-phase noise signals. Crystal oscillators leverage quartz crystals for high-frequency stability and precision, commonly used in communication systems. Other market segments may include digital oscillators and integrated circuit oscillators, catering to specific needs in various electronic and communication applications.</p></p>
