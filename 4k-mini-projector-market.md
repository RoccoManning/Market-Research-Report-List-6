<p><p>The 4K mini projector market features three primary technologies: LCD, DLP, and LCoS. LCD projectors utilize liquid crystal panels for vivid colors and sharp images, making them popular for presentations. DLP projectors use a digital light processor to create images from millions of micro-mirrors, offering high contrast and smooth motion, ideal for movies and gaming. LCoS combines liquid crystals and reflective technology for superior color accuracy and detail, making it suitable for home entertainment and professional use. Each technology caters to specific user preferences and applications.</p></p>
