<p><strong>The Surgical Loupes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through The Lens Loupe (TTL)</li><li>Flip Up Loupe</li><li>Galilean Loupe</li><li>Prismatic Loupe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical loupes are used by healthcare professionals to magnify their field of view during procedures. Through the Lens Loupe (TTL) integrates the magnification lens into the frame, providing a more compact design. Flip Up Loupes allow the user to flip the lens up when not in use. Galilean loupes use a series of lenses to achieve high magnification. Prismatic loupes use prisms to enhance image quality. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the surgical setting.</p></p>
