<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military night vision binoculars come in two main types: image intensifier and thermal image. Image intensifier binoculars amplify ambient light to create a clearer image in low-light conditions. They are ideal for situations where there is some light available, such as moonlight or starlight. Thermal image binoculars, on the other hand, detect heat signatures to create an image, allowing users to see objects even in complete darkness. This makes them especially useful for detecting camouflaged targets or in complete darkness.</p></p>
