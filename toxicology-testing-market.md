<p><p>Toxicology testing market consists of various types of technologies including cell culture technology, high throughput technologies, molecular imaging technologies, and omics technologies. Cell culture technology involves studying the effects of toxins on cells in a controlled environment. High throughput technologies allow for rapid screening of large numbers of compounds. Molecular imaging technologies can visualize the distribution of toxins in living organisms. Omics technologies analyze the effects of toxins on various biological molecules such as DNA, RNA, and proteins.</p></p>
