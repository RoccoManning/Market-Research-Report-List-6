<p><p>Wind power generators can be categorized into two main types: Horizontal-Axis Wind Power Generators and Vertical-Axis Wind Power Generators. Horizontal-axis generators are the most common type, where the rotor shaft and generator are parallel to the ground. Vertical-axis generators have the rotor shaft positioned vertically, allowing them to capture wind from any direction without the need for a yaw mechanism. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, and are used in various applications depending on factors like space availability and wind patterns.</p></p>
