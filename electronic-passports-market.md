Key trends include the integration of additional biometric features, such as fingerprints and iris scans, to bolster document authenticity. Moreover, collaborations between government agencies and technology providers are fostering innovation in passport production and validation processes. As countries continue to prioritize traveler security and convenience, the e-passport segment is anticipated to witness significant expansion, reshaping the future of international travel documentation.</p></p>
