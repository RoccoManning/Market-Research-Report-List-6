<p><p>Robotic palletizing and depalletizing systems are classified into two main types - full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic systems are fully autonomous and operate without the need for human intervention. They are ideal for high-volume and continuous production environments. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require some level of human interaction, such as loading/unloading items onto the system. These systems are more flexible and cost-effective for smaller scale operations. Both types of systems aim to increase efficiency, accuracy, and productivity in palletizing and depalletizing processes.</p></p>
