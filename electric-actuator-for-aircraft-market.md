<p><p>Electric actuators for aircraft can be categorized into two main types: linear motion and rotary motion. Linear motion actuators are utilized for applications that require straight-line movement, such as extending and retracting landing gear or moving control surfaces. On the other hand, rotary motion actuators are used for applications that require rotational movement, such as opening and closing valves or actuating flaps. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in the operation and functionality of various systems within an aircraft, ensuring safe and efficient performance.</p></p>
